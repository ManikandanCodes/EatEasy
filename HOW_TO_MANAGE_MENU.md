# How to Manage Menu

## Prerequisites
1. **Login** as a Restaurant Owner (e.g., `owner@eateasy.com`).
2. Ensure you are on the **Dashboard**.

## Step 1: Add Menu Categories
Before adding items (like "Chicken Burger"), you must create categories (like "Burgers", "Drinks").

1. Click on **"Manage Menu"** or navigate to the Menu section.
2. Click **"Categories"**.
3. Click **"Add Category"**.
4. Enter a name (e.g., "Starters") and click **Save**.
5. Repeat for other categories.

## Step 2: Add Menu Items
Once you have categories, you can add food items.

1. Click on **"Items"** in the Menu section.
2. Click **"Add Item"**.
3. Fill in the details:
   - **Name**: e.g., "Cheese Burger"
   - **Price**: e.g., 150
   - **Description**: e.g., "Juicy beef patty with cheese"
   - **Category**: Select "Burgers" from the dropdown.
   - **Veg/Non-Veg**: Check the box if applicable.
4. Click **"Add Item"**.

## Step 3: Verify
Go back to the **"Items"** list to see your newly added food items. They will now be visible to customers!
